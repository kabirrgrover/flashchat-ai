Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

FlashChat AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our text messaging service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Phone Number: Your mobile phone number when you text our service
  • Messages: The text messages you send to us
  • Images: Any images you upload for analysis
  • Conversation Data: The content of your conversations with our AI

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Timestamp Data: When messages are sent and received
  • Carrier Information: Your mobile carrier (provided by Twilio)
  • Device Information: Basic device type information from SMS metadata

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate our conversational AI service
  • Process your questions and analyze images you submit
  • Generate AI responses tailored to your queries
  • Improve our AI models and service quality
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Send you service-related messages (welcome messages, opt-out confirmations)

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 Service Providers

We share your information with trusted service providers who help us operate our service:

  • Twilio: SMS messaging platform that processes and delivers text messages
  • AI Service Providers: Third-party AI services that help process your queries and images
  • Cloud Hosting: Services that host our infrastructure

4.2 We Do NOT

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your information for third-party marketing purposes
  • Publicly post your private conversations or images

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal process or government requests
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Prevent fraud or illegal activity

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our service to you
  • Improve our AI models
  • Comply with legal obligations

If you opt out (text STOP), we will stop sending you messages but may retain historical conversation data for compliance and service improvement purposes.

6. Your Rights & Choices

6.1 Opt-Out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by texting STOP. You will receive a confirmation message.

6.2 Data Access & Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements)

To exercise these rights, text HELP for assistance or contact us as described below.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Access controls limiting who can view your data
  • Regular security assessments

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it.

9. Third-Party Links

Our responses may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to request deletion of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

Note: We do not sell your personal information.

11. International Users

Our service is based in the United States. If you are accessing our service from outside the U.S., please be aware that your information will be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last revised. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. AI-Specific Privacy Considerations

Because FlashChat AI uses artificial intelligence:

  • Your messages and images may be used to train and improve our AI models
  • AI-generated responses are created based on patterns learned from data
  • We implement safeguards to prevent AI from memorizing or reproducing sensitive personal information
  • Images you submit are analyzed by AI but not shared publicly

14. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices:

  • Text HELP to our service number
  • Visit our website at FlashChat AI

15. Consent

By using FlashChat AI, you consent to the collection, use, and sharing of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
