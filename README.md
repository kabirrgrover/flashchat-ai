# FlashChat AI

This repository hosts the static website for the FlashChat AI messaging service. FlashChat AI is an AI-powered conversational assistant that provides quick answers and image analysis for beauty, style, and product advice.

## Files

- `index.html` — The landing page for the service, including a clear explanation of what FlashChat AI does and instructions on how users can opt in to receive SMS messages. The page includes a call to action such as "Text JOIN to our number to start" and links to the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
- `terms.html` — The Terms of Service for FlashChat AI.
- `privacy.html` — The Privacy Policy for FlashChat AI.

The site is deployed via GitHub Pages at [https://kabirrgrover.github.io/flashchat-ai/](https://kabirrgrover.github.io/flashchat-ai/).

By texting the keyword **JOIN** to our number, users agree to receive automated messages from FlashChat AI (message and data rates may apply). Users can reply **STOP** to opt out at any time or **HELP** for assistance.
